# In This House

In this house, we treat each other with basic decency — no yelling, no "shut up," no contempt.

In this house, we use our words. When we can't, we walk away.

In this house, anyone can call a pause — that's what "pumpernickel" is for.

In this house, we play hard and fight fair — never with our hands out of anger.

In this house, every person under this roof is treated with respect, regardless of their role.

In this house, we carry our own weight.

In this house, big decisions get time. A rushed yes is worse than a patient no.

In this house, we say it when the tank is low — and we listen when someone else does.

In this house, adult problems stay between adults.

In this house, both your parents are in your corner. Always.
