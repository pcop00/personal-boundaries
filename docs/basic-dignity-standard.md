# Boundary: Basic Dignity Standard

## 1. The Line

**Yelling, "shut up," and coarse dismissal are not acceptable forms of communication in this household — from anyone, in any direction, regardless of frustration level.**

## 2. Rationale

This boundary was established to model the interpersonal baseline I want my kids to carry into their own lives. Everyone in this space is owed basic dignity. Frustration is valid; contempt expressed through volume or dismissal is not. This is not about perfection — it is about the floor.

## 3. Implementation

- **No Directional Exceptions:** The standard applies in every direction — kids to adults, adults to kids, adults to each other. Toughness or rank do not grant an exemption.
- **Frustration Has Other Outlets:** Feelings are valid. Yelling and dismissal are not a valid channel for them. Walking away, using words, or invoking the Interrupt Signal are the alternatives.
- **Consistency Over Mood:** The standard does not flex based on stress level, history, or who started it.

## 4. The Interrupt Signal

Either of the following words, spoken by anyone in the household, signals that the Basic Dignity Standard has been violated and the current interaction must pause immediately:

- **pumpernickel**
- **perpendicular**

When the signal is spoken:

1. The current exchange stops — no "but I was just..." and no counter-argument.
2. Everyone takes a physical step back and a breath.
3. The person who crossed the line acknowledges it simply: *"You're right. I'm sorry."*
4. The conversation may resume once the tone has reset.

The signal cannot be dismissed or argued. Whoever speaks it is always valid in doing so.

## 5. Communication (Signals)

- **[SIGNAL - The Interrupt]:** *(spoken by anyone)* "Pumpernickel." / "Perpendicular."
- **[SIGNAL - The Acknowledgment]:** "You're right. I'm sorry. Let me try that again."
- **[SIGNAL - The Reset]:** "Are we good? Can we start over?"

!!! warning "Internal Check"
    If the Interrupt Signal is directed at me, I do not argue or minimize it. The signal is correct by definition — the only valid response is to stop and reset.
