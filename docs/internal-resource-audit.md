# Boundary: Internal Resource Audit (The Capacity Check)

## 1. The Line

**I will not commit to any new expenditure of time, money, or emotional labor without first auditing my current "Resource Tank" and confirming a 20% surplus.**

## 2. Rationale

My default setting is to prioritize external needs to keep the peace. This leads to "Silent Burnout," where I am functionally operational but internally depleted. By requiring a 20% "Safety Buffer," I ensure that I always have enough reserves to handle an emergency with my kids without breaking.

## 3. Implementation

- **The "24-Hour Review" Default:** This activates BND-OPS-003 for *every* request, even "fun" ones. No "Yes" is delivered in the moment.
- **The Hard-Stop Sunday:** Sunday evenings are a "Read-Only" period. No planning, no heavy chores, no stressful texts. This is a manual reboot for the upcoming week.
- **The "Capacity Filter" Questions:** Before agreeing to help or show up, I must answer:
    1. Does this take away from my kids or my sleep?
    2. Am I doing this because I *want* to or because I'm afraid of the *friction* of saying no?
    3. If I say yes, what am I saying "No" to in my own life?

## 4. Communication (Signals)

- **[SIGNAL - Internal]:** "I feel the urge to say yes right now just to make this person happy. That is a System Alert. I am invoking the 24-hour buffer."
- **[SIGNAL - External]:** "That sounds like a great cause/event, but I've reached my 'people capacity' for this week. I need to stay home and recharge so I can be 100% for my kids tomorrow."
- **[SIGNAL - The Correction]:** "I previously said I could help with that, but I realized I overcommitted myself. I'm going to have to scale back my involvement to [Specific Limit] to keep my own life balanced."

## 5. Decision Logic: The Resource Tank

| Tank Level | Action Protocol |
| :---- | :---- |
| **High (>80%)** | Proceed with standard "Decision Latency" (BND-OPS-003). |
| **Medium (50–80%)** | One-in, One-out. (To do something new, cancel something else). |
| **Low (<50%)** | **Hard Freeze.** No new commitments. Cancel non-essential tasks. |

!!! warning "Internal Enforcement"
    If I find myself feeling resentful toward a person I just helped, it is a sign that I bypassed this audit. I must immediately enter "Maintenance Mode" (Solitude/Rest) to prevent a total system failure.
