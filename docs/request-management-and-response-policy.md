# Boundary: Request Management & Response Policy (SLA)

## 1. The Line

**I do not provide immediate consent or responses to non-emergency requests; all significant decisions and communications follow a mandatory "Triage and Buffer" protocol.**

## 2. Rationale

To eliminate "Conflict Avoidance Bias" (agreeing just to keep the peace) and to prevent "Notification Fatigue." This policy ensures that my responses are based on capacity and facts, not emotional pressure or the desire for a "quick fix" to a conversation.

## 3. The Triage & Response Table (The SLA)

| Priority | Definition | Response Window | Decision Buffer |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **P1: Critical** | Immediate physical danger or acute health crisis. | < 15 Minutes | None (Act Now) |
| **P2: High** | Schedule changes for the *next 24–48 hours*. | 4–8 Hours | 1-hour "Cool-off" |
| **P3: Normal** | General logistics, future planning, or family info. | 24–48 Hours | **Mandatory 24-hour buffer** |
| **P4: Low** | Non-essential updates or "FYIs." | Next Weekly Sync | N/A |
| **Blocked** | Emotional venting, theological debates, or critiques. | Null (No Response) | N/A |

## 4. Implementation (The "Anti-Appeasement" Protocol)

### 4.1 The "Wait-Out" Rule

- **The 24-Hour Buffer:** For any P3 request involving my time, money, or emotional labor, I must wait at least 24 hours before confirming.
- **Channel Integrity:** If a P3 message is sent via a P1 channel (e.g., a text about a bill next month), I will move the conversation to the appropriate P3 channel (Email/App) and reset the clock.

### 4.2 The Decision Flowchart

When a request enters the system:

1. **Identify Priority:** Is this a P1 (Medical/Safety)?
2. **Apply Buffer:** If not P1, do not commit. State the "Intake Signal."
3. **Internal Audit:** Write down the "External Argument" vs. my "Internal Capacity."
4. **Final Response:** Deliver a "Yes" or "No" only after the buffer has expired.

## 5. Communication (Signals)

- **[SIGNAL - The Intake]:** "I hear your request. Since this isn't an emergency, I'm going to take 24 hours to check my schedule and priorities before giving you an answer."
- **[SIGNAL - The Pressure Valve]:** "I'm not in a position to make a snap decision. If you need an answer immediately, it will have to be a 'No' so I don't overcommit."
- **[SIGNAL - The Channel Reset]:** "I see your message about [Topic]. I'm moving this to email so I can give it proper attention during my logistics window tomorrow."

!!! warning "Internal Enforcement"
    If I feel a physical "urge to appease" (tight chest, desire to stop the other person from being upset), that is a System Alert. I must exit the conversation or hang up until the buffer period is over.
