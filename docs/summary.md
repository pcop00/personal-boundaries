# PBR Summary — Version 1.1.0

## 1. Co-Parenting & Autonomy (BND-REL-001)

- **Audience:** Ex-Wife.
- **Boundary:** Relationship is primarily a co-parenting partnership. Proactive personal, logistical, or emotional support will not be provided.
- **[SIGNAL]:** "I'm focusing on the kids' needs right now. If this isn't about them, I'll have to get back to you later (or not at all)."

## 2. Information Disclosure (BND-REL-002)

- **Audience:** Family / Church Community.
- **Boundary:** Factual, logistical updates only (the "What" and "When"). Moral, theological, or "inner-working" causes are off limits.
- **[SIGNAL]:** "I appreciate you being a listening ear, but I'm only sharing the logistics of this process, not the 'why.' I hope you can respect that."

## 3. Request Management & Response / SLA (BND-OPS-003)

- **Audience:** All External Stakeholders.
- **Boundary:** Mandatory 24-hour buffer for all non-emergency decisions. Triage by priority (P1: Critical to P4: Low).
- **[SIGNAL]:** "I hear your request. I'm going to take 24 hours to check my schedule and priorities before giving you an answer."

## 4. Conflict Isolation / Zero-Audience (BND-REL-004)

- **Audience:** Ex-Wife (in presence of kids).
- **Boundary:** No verbal conflict, elevated tones, or sarcasm within earshot of the children. High-friction topics must move to asynchronous channels or a later time.
- **[SIGNAL]:** "The kids are right here. I am stopping this conversation now. We can discuss this via email tomorrow morning."

## 5. Physical Integrity & Interactive Play (BND-CORE-005)

- **Audience:** Kids.
- **Boundary:** Physical contact is for "Play" (fun), not "Reaction" (frustration). I am not a "tough" target for anger; intent matters more than impact.
- **[SIGNAL]:** "Hands off. I don't play when we're angry. I'm too important to be hit out of anger. We'll play again when we're calm."

## 6. Internal Resource Audit (BND-CORE-006)

- **Audience:** Self / Internal.
- **Boundary:** Maintain a 20% "Safety Buffer" of energy. No new commitments when the "tank" is low. Sunday evenings are marked for recovery.
- **[SIGNAL]:** "I've reached my 'people capacity' for this week. I need to stay home and recharge so I can be 100% for my kids."

## 7. Disclosure & Heartbeat (BND-REL-003.1)

- **Audience:** Current Partner.
- **Boundary:** Divorce updates limited to one "Daily Status Report." 24-hour observation (no advice) for hard shares. Priority on high-frequency assurance.
- **[SIGNAL]:** "You are non-negotiable. Full stop. I'm invoking the 24-hour rule for this update — I just need you to hear it for now."
