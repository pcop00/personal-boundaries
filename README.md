# PBR One Pager

1. **Co-Parenting & Autonomy (Ex-Wife):** Our relationship is now a co-parenting partnership focused on the children, and I am stepping back from providing proactive personal or emotional support.
2. **Information Disclosure (Family/Church):** I provide factual, logistical updates on the divorce process, but discussions regarding moral, theological, or personal causes are off-limits.
3. **Request Management & Response / SLA (General):** I utilize a mandatory 24-hour buffer for all non-emergency decisions to ensure my responses align with my actual capacity and priorities.
4. **Conflict Isolation (Ex-Wife/Kids):** I will not participate in elevated tones or verbal conflict within earshot of the children, moving high-friction topics to a later, private time or digital channel.
5. **Physical Integrity (Kids):** Physical contact is reserved for fun and play, and I will not be a target for physical touch motivated by frustration or anger.
6. **Internal Resource Audit (Self):** I maintain a 20% energy buffer for my well-being and do not accept new commitments when my internal "tank" is low.
7. **Disclosure & Heartbeat (Partner):** Divorce updates are limited to one concise report per day, allowing for a 24-hour "observation only" period for hard shares while prioritizing regular emotional assurance.
